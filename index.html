A Few Words About Me

Greetings! And a warm welcome to my webpage.

I am a physicist by training, with a Ph.D in astrophysics, a data enthusiast and am currently working in the coding education sector — introducing the opportunity to learn coding within public libraries in Bangladesh.

I was previously a researcher in astrophysics at Durham University. During my PhD I was researching the galaxies at the centre of clusters of galaxies. More information about my research can be found on the Astrophysics section of this webpage.

My undergraduate studies were in Maths and Physics at the University of Warwick.

I am an avid reader, of both fiction and non-fiction. See my Goodreads profile here.

I like to do photography as a hobby. The World around me has always fascinated me (with the drive to understand how it works leading me toward the study of physics) and photography is all about capturing those wonders around us. Photography is also a satisfying blend of artistic (finding an eye pleasing scene) and technical considerations (considering the physics of the light and optics involved for example). Some of my photos can be seen in the photography section of this webpage.

If you would like to check out my Twitter page, my handle is @TGreenBookWorm.
